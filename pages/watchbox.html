Pokemon Generations

Pokémon Generations was a animated series that revisits each generation of the Pokémon video game series to shed new light on some timeless moments. From the earliest days in the Kanto region to the splendor of the Kalos region, go behind the scenes and witness Pokémon history with new eyes! These bite-sized adventures are about 3 to 5 minutes long, and the series was completed December 23, 2016.
